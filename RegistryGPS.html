PRIVACY & LEGAL

🇮🇳 LEGAL COMPLIANCE — INDIA

Indian IT Act 2000: RegistryGPS processes all data locally on your device. No photos, GPS data, or personal information is transmitted to any server.

PDPB (Personal Data Protection): You retain full ownership of your photos and location data. The app never sells or shares your data.

Permissions: Camera, Location, and Storage permissions are used only for core app functionality as declared. You can revoke permissions at any time via device Settings.

GPS Stamp Legal Use: GPS-stamped photos may be used as supplementary evidence. Consult a lawyer for evidentiary admissibility under the Indian Evidence Act.

RegistryGPS is free, open-source, and ad-free. Version 1.0.0
